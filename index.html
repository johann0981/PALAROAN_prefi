Frequently Asked Questions

What is taho?
Taho is a Filipino dessert made with silken tofu, sago pearls, and a sweet syrup. It's often served warm and enjoyed as a snack or a light meal.
What are the ingredients in taho?
 main ingredients are silken tofu, tapioca pearls (sago), and brown sugar syrup. Some sellers may add additional toppings like ube halaya (purple yam jam) or leche flan.
How much does a serving of taho cost?
Prices vary depending on location, size, and ingredients. A typical small serving might cost around ₱10-₱20.
Can I get it with other toppings?
Some vendors offer additional toppings like ube halaya, leche flan, or even chocolate syrup. It's best to inquire about available options.
